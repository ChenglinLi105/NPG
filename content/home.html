About Us

Welcome to the Neuramind Probe Group at Zhejiang Normal University. Our team is dedicated to pursuing two primary long-term goals: first, to understand the neural mechanisms underlying perceptual expertise in processing faces and Chinese characters; and second, to investigate how these mechanisms operate from perception to memory. To achieve these objectives, we employ behavioral experiments combined with advanced neuroimaging techniques, including EEG, TMS, and fMRI.

We believe in open science and regularly share our findings through publications, open-source software, and collaborations with partners.
